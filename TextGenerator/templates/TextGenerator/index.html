GroupMe Text Generator


This website uses the full GroupMe message history of our group chat to train a simple text generation model for each individual group member and every member combined. To test it out, choose a person and token type and hit the 'Generate' button to generate a message based on their message history.

The token type tells the model to generate messages one word at a time or one character at a time with the 'Word' and 'Character' options respectively.
